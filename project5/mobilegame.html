We listed the ratings for each of the popular mobile games for your reference.

Call of duty: a free-to-play shooter game developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Activision for Android and iOS. It was released on October 1, 2019.

Rating: four stars

Comments: TiMi Studio Group currently works on many games that they don't have time to invest on Call of duty deeply



Pokemon GO: Pokémon Go is a 2016 augmented reality mobile game, part of the Pokémon franchise, developed and published by Niantic in collaboration with Nintendo and The Pokémon Company for iOS and Android devices.

Rating: five stars

Comments: Pokemon Go has its uniqueness that connects deeply with the Japanese animation which makes it more popular around the world. With its cute character design, many younger generations who watch animations a lot like this game very much.



Genshin Impact: Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. It was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, iOS, and Android in 2020, and on PlayStation 5 in 2021, and is set for release on Nintendo Switch.

Rating: five stars

Comments: Genshin Impact has its flexibility since it can be played on mobile, PC, as well as console, making it very popular these days. The storytelling during the game is really impressive and attractive for people around the world.
