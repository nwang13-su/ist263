Welcome to the ultra-game world! Our website gives an insight of all types of games and reliable rating systems for you to choose the game that attracts you the most. We're also here for people who don't know video games but want to learn and explore the game world. You can find all different categories of video games. So, why don't we start now?

Let's begin by walking you through the definition of video games. Video games usually refer to the game that plays on the digital screen, including TV, computer, mobile phone or any other types of digital screens. Since the involvement of various sizes and functions of screen, the variation of video games increases tremendously. The general devices used to play video games include PCs (personal computers), laptops, tablets, TV sets, monitors, and virtual reality headsets.


See? Isn't it so interesting to get to know all the games? Don't you feel that you are in a different world already? Let's get more excited into learning some of the games under these categories.



Category by Devices


PC Games

PC games are the games played on personal computers(PCs). Many games are available for individuals to play on their PCs. The commeen PC games include: League of Legends, Minecraft, Destiny 2, God of War, Grand Theft Auto V, Eldon Ring, Fortnite.



Mobile Games

Can you believe that there are around 478 thousand of mobile games available on google play store? Many people feature the mobile games because of its flexibility, convenience, and afforability. Some popupar mobile games are Call of Duty: Mobile, Among Us, Genshin Impact, Hearthstone, Clash of Clans, Pokemon GO, PUBG Mobile, Clash Royale.

Web Games

Some people feel that downloading a game is troublesome and taking many spaces so they prefer web games. Web games are games played through the internet by using the web browser. If you have your laptop, tablet, or PCs, you can quickly try any web game you are interested! Some web games include: A Dark Room, Heroes of Might and Magic Online, GeoGuessr, Isleward, Powerline.io.



Console Games

Console games are a type of game that use controllers such as analogue joysticks. The image and sound of the games are usually displayed on a TV or similar devices. Console games take up a huge market place in current days. Some console games are: ATetris, Overwatch, Red Dead Redemption 2, The Legend of Zelda.



VR Games

VR games stand for virtual reality games. It is a type of game using VR devices. Examples of VR games include: Beat Saber, Superhot VR, The Elder Scrolls V, The Walking Dead: Saints & Sinners.
